My name is Matt Brown.

Hi there! I'm Matt Brown from Ancaster, Ontario. I currently work as a GIS Specialist & Server Administrator at McMaster University. I have many years of experience creating maps, doing spatial data analysis, and teaching GIS to students. I have experience programming in different languages such as Python, R, and SQL. My favorite projects involve working with ArcGIS Desktop/Pro.

I graduated from McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario with a MSc. in Geography in April, 2020 after finishing my thesis - "Link-focused prediction of bike share trip volume using GPS data: A GIS based approach". I also graduated with an Honours Bachelor of Science in Environmental Sciences from McMaster University in June, 2017.
